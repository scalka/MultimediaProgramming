    DESIGN - PORTFOLIO

    DO

    - Tidy up your work, give it a layout and order, for example, it may be chronological or in thematic sections where initial work is placed next to final pieces,
    - Show your progress through period of time,
    - You can include your school projects and other colleges’ portfolio briefs,
    - Include different media such as photography, sketchbooks, collages, digital work (make sure the format you hand will display on other devices), 3d work (as a photography), storyboards, etc.,
    - Include finalized work as well as still in progress,
    - You can include a CV with all your awards, interests and hobbies.

    WE ARE

    LOOKING FOR:

    PEOPLE WHO...

    - Understands the course they are applying
    for (see iadt website for more details) ,
    - Are motivated and hard working,
    - Like to experiment, play with art,
    - Not necessary have technical or drawing skills,
    - Are curious and willing to learn new things,
    - Are creative and have orginal ideas.

    WITH PORTFOLIO...

    - Mainly demonstrating your abilities
    and interests of the specific course,
    - Which is an observation of how you
    interpret/observe surrounding you world,
    - Of selection of your best works.
